Snap@trix

Capture time in space!
  1. This event is open for students of classes VI-XII.
  2. Only 1 participant per school is allowed.
  3. Participants would have to depict a story line with the help of a series of photographs (containing 3 pictures) clicked on the theme given above.
  4. The pictures must be compiled into a collage having a JPG/JPEG format and must be numbered with a tagline for each photograph explaining the picture/idea of the story.
  5. Minimal editing/in-camera editing allowed on the images clicked.
  6. The entries should be shared via email to event heads.
  7. The participants must also provide the original photographs along with the edited version. The original photographs must be named ’Original1’, ’Original2’ and 'Original 3'.
  8. The participants will present their compilations in front of the judges on the final day.
