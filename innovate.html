INNOVATE@TRIX
Vulnerability is the birthplace of innovation creation and change.
  1. The event is open for classes IX-XII.
  2. Only 1 team consisting of 3 students can participate from each school, with one CEO, CFO and CTO each.
  3. The startup can adhere issues to any of the following topics.
  4. Each start-up must fall under the scope of one of the categories listed below. If a start-up fails to do so, the project will receive a less accurate evaluation and may even be penalised.:
    1. Social Start-Ups
    2. FMCG Start-Ups
    3. Financial Start-Ups
    4. Tech Start-Ups
    5. Surprise Us
  5. Referencing online materials – for understanding the topic, providing proof for a statement or any other purpose – is allowed; however, plagiarism will not be tolerated; if found, then the participant would be disqualified and their institution will receive a letter from our team describing their malpractice and low sense of academic integrity.

    6. Qualification Round

  6. The qualification round will begin from 19th July and will end on 20th July.
  7. The list of qualifiers would be uploaded on the event website on 21st July at 12pm.
  8. Participants have to create a powerpoint presentation explaining their startup idea.
  9. The powerpoint must have at max 10 slides covering the following points:
    1. Executive Summary
    2. Target Market
    3. Competitors
    4. Operations

    10. Final Round

  10. The participants must create an infomercial of their product along with a company name and logo. Use of templates is not allowed. Any qualifying team found using so shall be disqualified.
  11. Participants will have to pitch their startup company to the investors on a scheduled zoom call. You will have 10 minutes to present your complete idea including the infomercial, financial projections and marketing plan.
  12. This will be followed by a round of cross questioning on several aspects. The participants should try their best to convince the judges to invest in their company for a reasonable evaluation.
