Quiz@trix

A conference of Siddhartha Basu(s). Without Siddhartha Basu of course:)
  1. The event is open for students of classes IX-XII.
  2. Only 1 team consisting of 2 students can participate from each school.
  3. This event will be conducted in two phases: an Online Prelims (Crossword) followed by an Online Final Quiz.
  4. The date of prelims will be informed to the participants on their emails along with the link to take the prelims.
  5. Only top 4 high scoring teams will qualify for online Finals. The list of selected teams will be uploaded latest by 20th July 2020.
  6. Details of the final round will be shared with the qualified teams. Please be prepared that it will be a live round.
  7. Questions can be asked from any aspect of the world of technology and related industries.
