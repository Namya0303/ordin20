CODE@TRIX
Talk is cheap. Show me the code.
  1. The event is open for students of classes VI-XII.
  2. Only 1 team comprising of 2 students can participate from each school.
  3. A preliminary round will be conducted and max. of 10 teams will qualify for the onsite event on the basis of coding, creativity and design.
  4. Topic for the prelim will be uploaded on 8th July 2020. Participants have to submit their prelim entries by 15th July 2020.
  5. Results of the prelims will be declared by 20th July 2020.
  6. For the final round, to be held on 22nd July 2020, topic will be given on the spot.
  7. 2 PCs will be provided per team in the final round.
  8. Time provided for the onsite event : 3 hours
  9. Softwares provided will be Adobe Photoshop CS3, Sublime Text, Notepad++, Mozilla Firefox and Google Chrome
